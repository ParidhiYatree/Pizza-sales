# Pizza-sales
Identified top-selling pizza types, peak ordering time periods, and customer purchase patterns, helping improve product planning and sales strategy
•	Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze pizza sales performance, order trends, and revenue distribution.
•	Created DAX measures to calculate KPIs such as Total Revenue, Total Orders, and Average Order Value.
•	Designed visualizations to track sales by pizza size, category, time of order, and monthly trends.
•	Performed data cleaning and data modeling to ensure accurate and structured reporting.
Tools: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling, Data Visualization
